PickyPalette

A color picker that shows how much visual weight each color claims in your palette. The canvas maps a color space onto a plane — as you add colors, it splits into regions showing which color is closest at every point. Drag to pick a color and see its territory shift in real time.

Click the canvas or press C to enter pick mode. Drag to add and adjust a color. Use the axis switcher next to the slider to explore different slices of the color space. Paste hex values or sync live with design tools via Token Beam.
